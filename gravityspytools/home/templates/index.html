Welcome To Gravity Spy Tools!

This site is a sister site to Gravity Spy which is a Zooniverse project designed to combine the power of machine learning and citizen science to tackle big data problem such as the large ever-changing excess noise in LIGO gravitational wave data.

Here we will show a brief demonstration of how to utilize a similarity search within the Gravity Spy frame work. We hope that those who test this website will find it, first of all, useful, and secondly will contribute any and all suggestions for more tools they would like to see within this project!

This video should give the user the general idea of how one can utilize the tools on this website. A few things to keepin mind!

First, I would start off keeping queries to at most 150 for now. Keep in mind this is the beta of this interface and I would hate for anything to crash.

Second, when you preview your collection through using the similarity search, there are some results that come up blank as seen in the video. No worries, nothing is wrong here those are just links to the soon to be available VIRGO images! We have procesed almost all of them and are waiting for a few logistical things to happen before commencing the upload. At which point they will appear in this search as well as on the website.

Finally, sometimes after filtering your results in the Zooniverse collection interface, it takes a few seconds for those changes to propogate when you go back and ask for "Collection Metadata".
